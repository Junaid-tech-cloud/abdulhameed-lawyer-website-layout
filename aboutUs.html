About Us

OVERVIEW

Qawasmi Lawyers & Consultants was founded by Mr. Abdulhameed Al-Qawasmi in 2003. The founder has begun his legal studies, at Cairo University - Egypt in (1995 – 1999), before commencing his legal training in 2000. In 2003 Qawasmi lawyers & Consultants was first established in Amman – Jordan providing legal services to corporates and individuals. Mr. Al-Qawasmi has expanded his jurisdictions afterward to include each of Australia and New Zealand.

Abdulhameed represented his clients regionally and internationally. He advised and represented his high caliber clientele in varied areas including; purchasing companies through privatization; Petroleum companies on mega million dollar projects in the Middle East; investment and strategic planning; complex commercial litigation; and contractual matters.

Objective

We aim to provide a high standard and full legal service to all our clients. In Qawasmi Lawyers & Consultants, our main principle is to deliver our services with integrity, diligence, and professionalism.

Currently, we provide our clients with extended jurisdictional legal services nationally and internationally to cover Australia, New Zealand, Jordan, United Arab Emirates, Iraq, and Egypt. We are working to deliver a high standard of professional practice and to expand it geographically to cover all possible jurisdictions.

MISSION & VISION

We focus to deliver the highest standard professional and trusted legal services to assist our clients in operating their business/life sustainably. ​

We deliver valuable services in a timely and cost-effective way. We also live by what we preach—both inside our firm and in our community—by implementing sustainable business practices in every possible way, to help us, and our clients, to become driving forces toward a new economy.
